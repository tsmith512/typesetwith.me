About Typesetwith.me

Howdy

My name is Taylor Smith. I'm a web developer and graphic designer, and I love typography. I also like teaching others about typography, especially clients who want to learn more about how much care goes into the production of their pieces by creative professionals.

Inspiration

I absolutely love (and have referred many clients to) Tommi Kaikkonen's Interactive Guide to Blog Typography. However, I saw a few opportunities to take it a bit further by allowing visitors to:

  • See how these metrics interact with each other
  • Practice reading different samples in custom typesettings.
